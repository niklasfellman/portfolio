wordsearch
Find all the words! Designed to be easy to print directly from the browser. Part of a series of teaching tools.
clock game
Click on the correct clock! In swedish. Part of a series of teaching tools.
cellular automata
Elementary cellular automata. Play around with the 255 different possible configurations!
conways game of life
Two dimensional cellular automaton. The rules are as follows: 1. Any alive cell with two or three neighbors survive. 2. Any dead cell with three live neighbors becomes alive. 3. All other cells stay dead or dies.
brians brain
Two dimensional cellular automaton. Every cell can be in three states : Alive, dying or dead. Dead cells become alive if they have exactly two alive neighbors. Alive cells will go into the dying state and dying will go dead in the next generation.
snake
Eat the apples to grow longer! Run into a wall or yourself and you have to start over. Move with WASD or the arrow keys.
minesweeper
Can you find all the mines? Left click to reveal whats under a cell. The number tells you how many mines surrounds that cell. Right click will flag the cell for a possible mine. Flag all the mines to win or step on a mine to lose.
connect four
Two player connect four. Be the first to connect four bricks in a row, column or diagonal to win!
maze generator
Visualization of a maze generation algorithm. Uses backtracking and is made with html canvas.
